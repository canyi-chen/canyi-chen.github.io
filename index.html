Biography

Hi, my name is Canyi Chen (陈灿贻) and I am originally from Pingtan island, Fujian in China. I am currently a Postdoc Research Fellow in the Department of Biostatistics at the University of Michigan in Ann Arbor under the guidance of Professor Peter Song. I got my Ph.D. degree in statistics from the Institute of Statistics and Big Data at Renmin University of China (RUC) in June 2023. I am lucky to be advised by Professor Liping Zhu. Before the graduate school, I received my Bachelor in Mathematics from Beijing Normal University in 2018.

Research Interests

  1. Causal mediation analysis

  2. Conformal prediction

  3. Distributed algorithms

  4. High-dimensional inference / Posterior Inference
